### Revisiting offload test suite tiers

An RFC for running the offload tests under wine (llvm/offload-test-suite#1280)
brought up a discussion about the offload test suite's test tiers. We discussed
what the bar is for supporting new targets and configurations, as well as how
we want to communicate support levels. This largely led to the conclusion that
we should drop the "tiers" terminology and instead organize our support into
"pre-checkin CI", "nightly CI", and "best effort".
